## Project

Universe of Finance is an autonomous research framework that measures,
categorises, and forecasts the total volume of financial transactions across
the entire global economy — from DeFi to stock exchanges, consumer payments
to video game microtransactions.

## Key Concepts

- **Measure everything**: every financial transaction type in the global economy
- **TPS as primary metric**: transactions per second as the universal unit
- **Three scenarios**: baseline, high-growth, conservative projections for every category
- **Confidence tagging**: every estimate tagged 🟢 High / 🟡 Medium / 🔴 Low
- **Double-counting awareness**: explicit rules to avoid inflating totals
- **The Big Number**: the sum total of all global financial TPS
