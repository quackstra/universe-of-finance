# Equity Markets — Source Notes

## Primary Sources

### 1. World Federation of Exchanges (WFE)

**WFE FY 2024 Market Highlights Report**
- URL: https://www.world-exchanges.org/our-work/articles/wfe-market-highlights-fy-2024
- PDF: https://wfe-live.lon1.cdn.digitaloceanspaces.com/org_focus/storage/media/Cally%20Billimore/WFE%20FY%202024%20Market%20Highlights%20Report%2010022025.pdf
- Data type: Aggregated exchange-level statistics for all WFE members
- Key metrics used:
  - Number of trades (Dec 2024): 5,094,833 thousand, +26.53% YoY
  - Value of share trading (Dec 2024): $14,431,297 million, +29.42% YoY
  - Global equity market cap: USD 125.71 trillion (+13.4% vs 2023)
  - ~$155 trillion (EOB) annual trading through WFE members
- Confidence: 🟢 High — WFE is the gold standard for exchange-level data
- Notes: WFE data covers electronic order book (EOB) trading only. Negotiated deals and some OTC activity excluded.

**WFE H1 2024 Market Highlights**
- URL: https://www.world-exchanges.org/news/articles/wfe-data-trading-value-and-volumes-surge-investors-flock-markets
- Key metrics:
  - APAC region: 18.68 billion trades in H1 2024 (highest half-year in 5 years)
  - Equity trading value: +11.7% vs H2 2023, +9.71% vs H1 2023
  - Equity trading volumes: +9.6% vs H2 2023, +18.25% vs H1 2023
- Confidence: 🟢 High

**WFE December 2024 Dashboard**
- URL: https://focus.world-exchanges.org/issue/december-2024/dashboard
- Regional value traded (Oct 2024 monthly): Americas $6.57T, APAC $7.08T, EMEA $0.78T
- Confidence: 🟢 High

**WFE Regional Growth Rates (2024 vs 2023)**
- URL: https://www.world-exchanges.org/news/articles/wfe-fy24-market-data-americas-region-leading-global-markets-despite-lowest-level-ipos-worldwide-last-5-years
- Shenzhen SE: +21.4% (trades), +17.6% (value)
- Shanghai SE: +24.2% (trades), +19.1% (value)
- NSE India: +74.3% (trades), +76.5% (value)
- JPX: +30.9% (trades), +22.5% (value)
- HKEX: +31.1% (value)
- Taiwan SE: +41.6% (value)
- APAC total: +47.8% (trades)
- EMEA total: +4.8% (trades)
- Trading value overall: +15.5%
- Confidence: 🟢 High

### 5. NSE India

- URL: https://www.nseindia.com/
- Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Stock_Exchange_of_India
- Key metrics:
  - #3 globally in cash equities by number of trades (2023)
  - #1 globally in derivatives by number of contracts

  - Trade count growth: +74.3% YoY in 2024

- Confidence: 🟢 High — regulatory data

- Confidence: 🟢 High
