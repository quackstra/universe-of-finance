> Depth research conducted 2026-03-28 to improve confidence from 50 to a defensible level.

## New Data Points Found

| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|

| Returns audited | 505,514 | Same |

**Derived US tax transaction count**:
- Individual returns (tax payments): ~161M
- Business returns: ~10-12M (corporate, partnership, S-corp)
- Quarterly estimated payments: ~40M (estimated)
- Employer withholding deposits (941s): ~30M employers × 12 months = ~360M
- Refund disbursements: ~94M
- Installment payments: ~15-20M (estimated from $16B / ~$1,000 avg)
- **Total US tax-related payment transactions: ~680-690M/year**

This is more granular than the original "~250M tax payment transactions" estimate in the report's triangulation section. The employer withholding deposits alone add ~360M transactions.

### 2. US Social Security & Benefits — Hard Data

| Metric | Value | Source |

**Derived US benefits transaction count**:
- Social Security: 71M recipients × 12 months = **852M** disbursements/year
- SNAP/food stamps: ~42M recipients × 12 = **504M**
- Medicare payments to providers: ~1.5B claims/year (estimated)
- Unemployment insurance: ~6M average × 26 weeks avg = **156M**
- Veterans benefits: ~6M recipients × 12 = **72M**
- SSI (Supplemental Security Income): ~7.5M × 12 = **90M**
- **Total US benefits disbursements: ~3.2B/year**

Combined with tax transactions: **US government payment transactions: ~3.9B/year**

The original bottom-up model had US at 5.09B — our new figure of ~3.9B is modestly lower, suggesting the original was reasonable but possibly included more categories.
