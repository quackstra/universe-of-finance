Extended notes on each source consulted during research. Accessed 2026-03-26.

Primary Sources

1. Nilson Report — "Global Network Card Results Worldwide — 2024"

  • URL: https://nilsonreport.com/articles/global-network-card-results-worldwide-2024/
  • Key data: 772.73B purchase transactions in 2024 (+12.4% YoY); $51.920T total volume
  • Reliability: 🟢 High — Nilson Report is the gold standard for payment card industry data
  • Notes: Nilson tracks all six global networks (Visa, UnionPay, Mastercard, AmEx, JCB, Discover/Diners Club). Figures include consumer, small business, and commercial card products. Paywalled — figures extracted from search result snippets and secondary reporting.

2. Nilson Report — "Top 150 Merchant Acquirers Worldwide — 2024"

  • URL: https://nilsonreport.com/articles/top-150-merchant-acquirers-worldwide-2024/
  • Key data: 550.24B transactions processed by top 150 acquirers in 2024 (+6.1%)
  • Notes: This is acquirer-side data, not network-side. Lower than 772.73B because not all transactions go through the top 150 acquirers, and methodology differs.

3. Visa Inc. — Fiscal Year 2024 Earnings Release (Q4)

  • URL: https://s1.q4cdn.com/050606653/files/doc_financials/2024/q4/Q4-2024-Earnings-Release_vF.pdf
  • Key data: 233.8B processed transactions; 303B total payments and cash transactions; $6.02T debit volume; $5.31T credit volume
  • Reliability: 🟢 High — SEC-filed audited financial data
  • Fiscal year: Ends September 30, 2024

4. Visa Inc. — Annual Report 2024

  • URL: https://s29.q4cdn.com/385744025/files/doc_downloads/2024/Visa-Fiscal-2024-Annual-Report.pdf
  • Key data: 829 million transactions per day average; 303B total branded transactions
  • Reliability: 🟢 High

5. Visa Inc. — 10-K FY2024

  • URL: https://s1.q4cdn.com/050606653/files/doc_financials/2024/ar/2ddc584c-441d-475b-8a97-630dad75db94.pdf
  • Key data: Historical transaction volumes; VisaNet capacity of 83,000 TPS
  • Reliability: 🟢 High — SEC filing

6. Mastercard — FY2024 Earnings Release

  • URL: https://www.mastercard.com/global/en/news-and-trends/stories/2025/earnings-review-2024-and-the-road-ahead.html
  • Key data: 159.4B switched transactions (+11%); $9.8T GDV; $28.2B net revenue
  • Reliability: 🟢 High — public company earnings data
  • Calendar year: Ends December 31, 2024

7. Mastercard — 10-K FY2024

  • URL: https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1141391/000114139125000011/ma-20241231.htm
  • Key data: Detailed breakdown of switched transactions; tokenization at 4B/month
  • Reliability: 🟢 High — SEC filing

8. American Express — FY2024 Annual Report

  • URL: https://s26.q4cdn.com/747928648/files/doc_financials/2024/ar/v2/American-Express-2024-Annual-Report.pdf
  • Key data: $1,551B billed business; 83.6M proprietary cards; 62.9M third-party cards
  • Reliability: 🟢 High — SEC filing
  • Notes: AmEx does not prominently report transaction counts in the same way as Visa/MC. Volume data is primarily in dollar terms.

9. American Express — FY2024 Earnings Release

  • URL: https://ir.americanexpress.com/news/investor-relations-news/investor-relations-news-details/2025/American-Express-Announces-Record-FY-2024-Revenue-Up-9-or-10-on-an-FX-Adjusted-Basis/default.aspx
  • Key data: Revenue up 9% (10% FX-adjusted); record card acquisitions (13M new cards)
  • Reliability: 🟢 High

Secondary Sources

  • URL: https://capitaloneshopping.com/research/number-of-credit-card-transactions/
  • Key data: ~791B total global credit card transactions (2024); 90.3M per hour; 1,781/sec in US
  • Reliability: 🟡 Medium — aggregated/derived data from multiple primary sources
  • Notes: Their figure of 791B is slightly higher than Nilson's 772.73B, likely due to including private-label and domestic-only cards

11. Capital One Shopping Research — "Credit Card Market Share"

  • Reliability: 🟢 High — Federal Reserve survey data

Sources Consulted But Not Used

  • Statista — Multiple pages referenced but primary data behind paywall; figures cited in other secondary sources
  • Nilson Report archive — Paywall prevented access to full historic timeseries
  • Worldpay Global Payments Report — Referenced in search results but not directly accessed
  • McKinsey Global Payments Report 2025 — Referenced but paywalled

Data Gaps & Limitations

  1. Historic timeseries (2016-2021): Year-by-year global totals for 2016, 2018, 2019, and 2020 are interpolated, not from primary sources
  2. UnionPay 2024 transactions: Estimated from 2023 base + growth rate; UnionPay does not publish figures in the same format as Western networks
  3. Peak TPS: No network publicly discloses actual observed peak TPS; all peak figures are estimates
  4. Credit/debit/prepaid split: Global breakdown is estimated; precise figures available only for US market
  5. Regional breakdown: Percentages from Payments Dive/Nilson; absolute figures derived
  6. AmEx/JCB/Discover transaction counts: Not prominently reported; estimated from combined figure and relative sizing
