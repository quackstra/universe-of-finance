S2: Worldpay Global Payments Report 2025 (GPR25)

  • Title: "10 Years of Cash, Cards and Crypto: Worldpay's Global Payments Report"
  • URL: https://worldpay.com/en/global-payments-report
  • PDF: https://offers.worldpayglobal.com/rs/850-JOA-856/images/GPR25.pdf
  • Accessed: 2026-03-26
  • Data used: E-commerce payment method shares (2024), digital payments growth trajectory
  • Key findings:
  • Digital wallets: 53% of e-commerce spend (2024)
  • Credit cards: 20%
  • Debit cards: 12%
  • Bank transfer / A2A: 7%
  • BNPL: 5%
  • Cash on delivery: 2%
  • Other: 1%
  • Digital payments grew from 34% to 66% of e-commerce value between 2014-2024
  • BNPL online spending grew from $2.2B (2014) to $342B (2024)
  • A2A e-commerce spend projected to hit $936B by 2030, from $152B in 2014
  • Confidence: 🟢 High — primary research across 40+ countries, industry standard
