# CEX — Source Notes

## Primary Sources

### CoinGecko Annual Reports
- Most reliable aggregator for CEX spot volume
- Applies trust scoring methodology to filter low-quality exchanges
- Top-15 exchange data available from 2020-2024
- URL: https://www.coingecko.com/research/publications/2024-annual-crypto-report

### CoinGecko Perpetuals Report
- First comprehensive annual derivatives volume report
- Covers top 10 CEXs by perpetual futures volume
- $58.5T figure for 2024 is the headline number
- URL: https://assets.coingecko.com/reports/2025/CoinGecko-State-of-Crypto-Perpetuals-Market.pdf

### TokenInsight
- Independent exchange analytics firm
- Provides Q-by-Q breakdowns and market share analysis
- Confirms Binance dominance declining from 42.2% to 32.7% over 2024
- URL: https://tokeninsight.com/en/research/reports/crypto-exchange-2024-annual-report

## Limitations of Sources

1. **No trade count data exists publicly** — all volume is in USD terms
2. **Self-reported exchange data** — even CoinGecko relies on exchange APIs that can be manipulated
3. **Derivatives data is newer** — historical derivatives volume series before 2023 is incomplete
4. **Regional exchanges are undercounted** — Korean (Upbit, Bithumb) and Japanese exchanges may be underrepresented in global aggregators

## Cross-Validation
- CoinGecko ($18.83T spot) aligns with CryptoSlate ($18T+), Finance Magnates reporting
- TokenInsight confirms Binance at ~39% share
- Derivatives figures from CoinGecko ($58.5T) and Bitget research ($58.5T) converge
- The ~$77T combined figure aligns with Binance Square reporting of $75.8T and CryptoSlate's $80T figure
