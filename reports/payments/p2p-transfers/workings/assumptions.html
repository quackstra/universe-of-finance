Peer-to-Peer Transfers — Assumptions

Key Assumptions

1. Category Boundary — P2P vs. Digital Wallets

  • UPI (India) is classified under Digital Wallets even though much of its volume is P2P, because UPI is a platform-level payment rail, not a dedicated P2P app
  • WeChat Pay / Alipay P2P similarly classified under Digital Wallets
  • This means this category is heavily US-weighted (~90% of volume)
  • If UPI P2P were included, this category would be 10-20x larger

2. Venmo Transaction Count

  • PayPal does not separately disclose Venmo transaction counts
  • Estimated using: TPV ($280B) / assumed average transaction size ($120)
  • The $120 average is derived from industry reports and the fact that Venmo skews younger with smaller transactions than Zelle ($289 avg)
  • Uncertainty: +/- 30%

3. Cash App Scope

  • Block reports "Cash App inflows" ($283B) which includes:
  • P2P transfers (~60%)
  • Direct deposits (~25%)
  • Bitcoin purchases (~10%)
  • Cash App Pay merchant transactions (~5%)
  • Pure P2P estimated at ~$170B
  • Transaction count estimate uses the full inflow figure divided by estimated avg size

4. Non-US P2P

  • European P2P data from Bizum (Spain, ~150M users, ~$30B annual) and Swish (Sweden, ~8M users, ~$15B annual) are the most transparent
  • Other European P2P (TWINT Switzerland, Paylib France, etc.) estimated at ~$40B combined
  • Brazil's PIX has massive P2P volume (~$1.5T in 2024) but is classified under Bank Transfers in this taxonomy because PIX is a central bank payment rail
  • If PIX P2P were included here, non-US would dominate this category

5. Average Transaction Sizes

  • Zelle: $289 (derived from official data: $1.04T / 3.6B txns)
  • Venmo: ~$120 (industry estimate — social/small payments skew)
  • Cash App: ~$115-190 (range depending on P2P-only vs. all inflows)
  • These are increasing over time as P2P expands into rent, utilities, small business

6. Value Jump 2023-2024

  • The aggregate value jumps from $1.45T to $2.8T, which looks discontinuous
  • This is partly because the 2024 figure includes Cash App total inflows ($283B) while earlier years used more conservative P2P-only estimates
  • Also driven by Zelle's $234B YoY jump ($806B → $1,040B)

7. Projection Assumptions

  • Baseline: US market growth decelerates to single digits by 2030; non-US P2P grows at 15-20%
  • High Growth: P2P replaces checks and small wire transfers; becomes primary method for freelancer payments
  • Conservative: Regulatory headwinds (CFPB scrutiny of fraud on Zelle), market saturation
