# OTC Derivatives — Source Notes

## Primary Sources

### BIS OTC Derivatives Statistics (Semiannual)
- **Authority**: Gold standard for notional outstanding and gross market values
- **Methodology**: Reports from 65 major dealers in 12 jurisdictions, published every 6 months
- **Coverage**: All OTC derivative asset classes (IR, FX, credit, equity, commodity)
- **Metrics**: Notional outstanding, gross market value, gross credit exposure
- **Limitation**: Does NOT report trade counts or turnover frequency
- **URL**: https://www.bis.org/statistics/derstats.htm

### BIS Triennial Central Bank Survey (Triennial)
- **Authority**: Most comprehensive turnover data for OTC derivatives
- **Methodology**: Survey of ~1,200 dealers in 52 jurisdictions, conducted every 3 years in April
- **Coverage**: OTC interest rate derivatives and FX derivatives (turnover)
- **Metrics**: Daily turnover (notional amounts, net-net basis)
- **2025 results**: $7.9T/day OTC IRD, $6.6T/day OTC FX derivatives
- **URL**: https://www.bis.org/statistics/rpfx25.htm

### ISDA SwapsInfo
- **Authority**: Industry body; aggregates DTCC SDR data
- **Methodology**: Weekly and quarterly reports from US, EU, UK trade repositories
- **Coverage**: IRD and CDS transactions reported under CFTC, EMIR, and FCA rules
- **Metrics**: Trade count, traded notional, SEF vs. non-SEF, cleared vs. uncleared
- **Limitation**: Covers only US/EU/UK markets; does not cover equity or commodity OTC derivatives
- **Key data**: ~2.9M IRD trades in 2024; ~317K CDS trades
- **URL**: https://analysis.swapsinfo.org/

### ISDA Key Trends Reports
- **Authority**: ISDA analysis of BIS semiannual data
- **Coverage**: Global OTC derivatives by asset class, notional outstanding
- **Published**: Semiannually, shortly after BIS data release
- **URL**: https://www.isda.org/category/research/market-analysis/

## Secondary Sources

### DTCC (Depository Trust & Clearing Corporation)
- **Role**: Operates swap data repositories (SDR) and Trade Information Warehouse
- **Coverage**: US trade reporting (CFTC), global CDS data
- **Metrics**: Weekly transaction activity, reference entity data
- **URL**: https://www.dtcc.com/repository-otc-data

### LCH (London Clearing House)
- **Role**: World's largest OTC derivatives CCP
- **Coverage**: Interest rate swaps, CDS, FX NDFs
- **Metrics**: Clearing volumes, compression volumes
- **Relevance**: Indicator of cleared vs. bilateral activity

## Data Gaps
- No single source reports global OTC derivative TRADE COUNTS across all asset classes
- Equity OTC derivatives: notional outstanding from BIS, but no trade count data
- Commodity swaps: minimal trade-level transparency
- EM and Asian markets: trade reporting rules vary; data availability is inconsistent
- BIS triennial only covers IRD and FX — no turnover data for equity, credit, or commodity OTC derivatives
