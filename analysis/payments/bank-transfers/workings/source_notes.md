# Source Notes — Bank Transfers (ACH/RTGS)

## Primary Sources

### 1. Nacha (US ACH Network)
- **URL**: https://www.nacha.org/content/ach-network-volume-and-value-statistics
- **Data**: ACH Network volume and value statistics 2015-2025
- **Reliability**: Direct operator of the ACH Network. Primary source.
- **Key stats**: 33.6B payments, $86.2T value in 2024; 35.19B payments, $93T in 2025 (full year)
- **Same Day ACH**: 1.2B transactions, $3.2T value in 2024 (45.3% YoY growth)
- https://www.nacha.org/news/same-day-ach-passes-major-milestone-2024-ach-network-shows-higher-growth
- https://www.nacha.org/news/nacha-releases-top-50-financial-institution-ach-originators-and-receivers-2024-total-ach

### 2. Federal Reserve — Fedwire Funds Service
- **URL**: https://www.frbservices.org/resources/financial-services/wires/volume-value-stats/annual-stats.html
- **Data**: Annual Fedwire transaction volume and value, 1987-2025
- **Reliability**: Official Fed data. Gold standard.
- **Key stats (2024)**: 209.9M transactions, $1.133 quadrillion total value, avg daily volume 836,322, avg daily value $4.516T
- **Key stats (2023)**: 193.3M transactions, $1.087 quadrillion total value

### 3. Federal Reserve — FedNow Service
- **URL**: https://www.frbservices.org/resources/financial-services/fednow/quarterly-volume-value-stats
- **Data**: Quarterly FedNow volume/value since launch (July 2023)
- **Reliability**: Official Fed data.
- **Key stats (2024)**: ~1.5M total transactions, $38.2B total value. Q4 2024: 915,263 txns (2,000% YoY growth)
- Still nascent but growing explosively.

### 4. ECB — TARGET2/T2 and Euro Payments
- **URL**: https://www.ecb.europa.eu/paym/target/t2/facts/html/index.en.html
- **URL**: https://www.ecb.europa.eu/press/stats/paysec/html/ecb.pis2024h2~5ada0087d2.en.html
- **Data**: T2 RTGS transaction volumes; Euro area non-cash payment statistics
- **Reliability**: Official ECB data. Primary source.
- **T2 (2024)**: 108M transactions (record high), avg daily 421,875
- **Euro credit transfers H1 2024**: 15.7B transactions, EUR 105.6T value
- **Euro credit transfers H2 2024**: ~16.3B transactions (21% of 77.6B total non-cash)
- **Full year 2024 credit transfers**: ~32B transactions, ~EUR 220T value

### 5. NPCI — India UPI
- **URL**: https://www.npci.org.in/product/upi/product-statistics
- **Data**: Monthly UPI transaction volumes and values
- **Reliability**: Official operator data. Primary source.
- **Key stats (2024)**: 131.1B transactions (46% YoY growth)
- **Key stats (2023)**: 83.7B transactions
- **Key stats (2025 annualized)**: 228.3B transactions (on track)
- **IMF recognition**: UPI accounts for 49% of global real-time payment transactions

### 6. Banco Central do Brasil — PIX
- **URL**: https://www.bcb.gov.br/en
- **Data**: PIX transaction statistics
- **Reliability**: Official central bank data.
- **Key stats (2024)**: ~63-69B transactions (sources vary; using 63B as conservative midpoint), ~$3.8-5T value
- **Peak single day**: 252.1M transactions (Dec 20, 2024)
- **76.4% of Brazil's population** uses PIX

### 7. ACI Worldwide — Prime Time for Real-Time Report (2024 edition)
- **URL**: https://www.aciworldwide.com/real-time-payments-report
- **URL**: https://investor.aciworldwide.com/news-releases/news-release-details/global-real-time-payments-growth-sustainable-new-use-cases-push
- **Data**: Global real-time payments benchmarking across 80+ countries
- **Reliability**: Industry report with central bank data. High reliability.
- **Key stats (2023)**: 266.2B global real-time payments (42.2% YoY growth)
- **Forecast (2028)**: 575.1B transactions (16.7% CAGR 2023-2028)
- **Regional (2023)**: APAC 185.8B, India 129.3B, Brazil 37.4B

### 8. SWIFT
- **URL**: https://www.swift.com/about-us/discover-swift/fin-traffic-figures
- **Data**: Daily FIN message volumes
- **Reliability**: Official SWIFT data.
- **Key stats (2024)**: Avg 53.3M messages/day (~13.3B annually at ~250 business days)
- **GPI**: 4,000+ banks, $300B/day, 92% credited within 24 hours

### 9. Bank of England / Pay.UK
- **URL**: https://www.wearepay.uk/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/Annual-Statistics-2024-v1.2.pdf
- **URL**: https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/report/2025/rtgs-and-chaps-annual-report-2024-25
- **Data**: UK payment systems (CHAPS, Faster Payments, BACS)
- **Reliability**: Official UK data.
- **CHAPS (2024)**: 52.7M transactions
- **Faster Payments (2024)**: 5.1B transactions, GBP 4.2T value

### 10. CIPS — China Cross-Border
- **URL**: https://www.cips.com.cn/en/about_us/about_cips/introduction/index.html
- **Data**: CIPS annual transaction volume and value
- **Reliability**: Official CIPS data, corroborated by PBOC reports.
- **Key stats (2024)**: 8.22M transactions, RMB 175.49T ($24.47T) value
- **Growth**: 24.25% volume, 42.60% value YoY

### 11. McKinsey Global Payments Report
- **URL**: https://www.mckinsey.com/industries/financial-services/our-insights/global-payments-report
- **Data**: Global payments market sizing, A2A shift analysis
- **Reliability**: Top-tier consulting firm research. Medium-high reliability.
- **Key insight**: Transaction mix shifting from credit cards toward lower-yield A2A payments
- **3.4 trillion** total global payment transactions in 2023

### 12. Federal Reserve Payments Study
- **URL**: https://www.federalreserve.gov/paymentsystems/fr-payments-study.htm
- **Data**: US noncash payment data (CY 2021/2022)
- **Reliability**: Official Fed research. Primary source.
- **Key insight**: Commercial ACH grew faster 2018-2021 (8.2% p.a.) than 2015-2018 (4.8% p.a.)
