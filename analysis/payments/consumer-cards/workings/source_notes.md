## Primary Sources

### 1. Nilson Report — "Global Network Card Results Worldwide — 2024"
- **URL:** https://nilsonreport.com/articles/global-network-card-results-worldwide-2024/
- **Key data:** 772.73B purchase transactions in 2024 (+12.4% YoY); $51.920T total volume
- **Reliability:** 🟢 High — Nilson Report is the gold standard for payment card industry data
- **Notes:** Nilson tracks all six global networks (Visa, UnionPay, Mastercard, AmEx, JCB, Discover/Diners Club). Figures include consumer, small business, and commercial card products. Paywalled — figures extracted from search result snippets and secondary reporting.

### 2. Nilson Report — "Top 150 Merchant Acquirers Worldwide — 2024"
- **URL:** https://nilsonreport.com/articles/top-150-merchant-acquirers-worldwide-2024/
- **Key data:** 550.24B transactions processed by top 150 acquirers in 2024 (+6.1%)
- **Notes:** This is acquirer-side data, not network-side. Lower than 772.73B because not all transactions go through the top 150 acquirers, and methodology differs.

### 3. Visa Inc. — Fiscal Year 2024 Earnings Release (Q4)
- **URL:** https://s1.q4cdn.com/050606653/files/doc_financials/2024/q4/Q4-2024-Earnings-Release_vF.pdf
- **Key data:** 233.8B processed transactions; 303B total payments and cash transactions; $6.02T debit volume; $5.31T credit volume
- **Reliability:** 🟢 High — SEC-filed audited financial data
- **Fiscal year:** Ends September 30, 2024

### 4. Visa Inc. — Annual Report 2024
- **URL:** https://s29.q4cdn.com/385744025/files/doc_downloads/2024/Visa-Fiscal-2024-Annual-Report.pdf

### 5. Visa Inc. — 10-K FY2024

## Data Gaps & Limitations

1. **Historic timeseries (2016-2021):** Year-by-year global totals for 2016, 2018, 2019, and 2020 are interpolated, not from primary sources
2. **UnionPay 2024 transactions:** Estimated from 2023 base + growth rate; UnionPay does not publish figures in the same format as Western networks
3. **Peak TPS:** No network publicly discloses actual observed peak TPS; all peak figures are estimates
4. **Credit/debit/prepaid split:** Global breakdown is estimated; precise figures available only for US market
5. **Regional breakdown:** Percentages from Payments Dive/Nilson; absolute figures derived
6. **AmEx/JCB/Discover transaction counts:** Not prominently reported; estimated from combined figure and relative sizing
