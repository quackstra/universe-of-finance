Tokenised Real-World Assets — Source Notes

Extended notes on each source consulted during research. Accessed 2026-03-26.

Primary Sources

1. RWA.xyz — Analytics on Tokenized Real-World Assets

  • URL: https://app.rwa.xyz/
  • Key data: Real-time TVL tracking across RWA protocols; $24B+ on-chain TVL mid-2025; $402.5B off-chain backing; asset class breakdowns
  • Reliability: :green_circle: High — industry-standard tracker, on-chain data verifiable
  • Notes: The go-to dashboard for RWA metrics. Excludes stablecoins from primary TVL figures. Tracks private credit, treasuries, commodities, real estate separately.

2. The Defiant — RWAs Became Wall Street's Gateway to Crypto in 2025

  • URL: https://thedefiant.io/news/defi/rwas-became-wall-street-s-gateway-to-crypto-in-2025
  • Key data: On-chain RWAs tripled from $5.5B to $18.6B over 2025; BlackRock BUIDL at $1.8B; RWA TVL overtook DEX TVL
  • Reliability: :yellow_circle: Medium — respected crypto media, cross-references on-chain data
  • Notes: Good narrative overview of the TradFi-to-DeFi convergence thesis.

3. Yahoo Finance / CoinDesk — RWA DeFi Protocols Overtake DEXs in TVL

  • URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/news/real-world-asset-rwa-defi-201504989.html
  • Key data: RWA TVL surpassed DEX TVL in 2025; symbolic milestone for institutional DeFi
  • Reliability: :yellow_circle: Medium
  • Notes: Milestone indicator rather than primary data source.

4. RedStone — Real-World Assets in Onchain Finance Report

  • URL: https://blog.redstone.finance/2025/06/26/real-world-assets-in-onchain-finance-report/
  • Key data: Comprehensive analysis of RWA TVL reaching $24B+ by mid-2025; private credit at $17B (61%)
  • Reliability: :yellow_circle: Medium — RedStone is a DeFi oracle provider with deep on-chain analytics
  • Notes: Best single comprehensive report on RWA state as of mid-2025.

5. CoinGecko — 2025 RWA Report

  • URL: https://assets.coingecko.com/reports/2025/CoinGecko-2025-RWA-Report.pdf
  • Key data: Market analysis and protocol comparisons for RWA tokens
  • Reliability: :yellow_circle: Medium
  • Notes: Good for protocol-level detail.

Projection Sources

6. BCG / Multiple — $16-30T by 2030

  • Key data: BCG estimates $16T by 2030 in base case, up to $30T in bull case
  • Reliability: :yellow_circle: Medium — reputable consultancy but projections in nascent markets are inherently speculative
  • Notes: These projections are frequently cited across the industry. They assume broad regulatory clarity and institutional adoption.

7. 21.co — $3.5-10T by 2030

  • Key data: Baseline $3.5T, bullish $10T by 2030
  • Reliability: :yellow_circle: Medium
  • Notes: More conservative than BCG. 21.co is a digital asset management firm (parent of 21Shares).

8. Citigroup — $4-5T by 2030

  • Key data: Institutional forecast from Citi's digital assets research team
  • Reliability: :yellow_circle: Medium — major bank research, but RWA projections are uncertain
  • Notes: Citi is one of the more conservative institutional forecasters.

9. Standard Chartered — $30T by 2034

  • Key data: Most bullish major bank forecast
  • Reliability: :red_circle: Low — extremely aggressive projection
  • Notes: Assumes tokenisation becomes the default for all new bond issuances.

10. NextMSC — $9.4T by 2030 (72.8% CAGR)

  • URL: https://www.nextmsc.com/report/tokenized-real-world-assets-rwas-market-bf3345
  • Key data: Market research firm projection with specific CAGR
  • Reliability: :red_circle: Low — market research firms tend to overproject in nascent categories

Data Gaps

  1. On-chain RWA transaction counts — no aggregator tracks this specifically. Our estimates are derived from TVL ratios.
  2. Secondary market trading volume for tokenised assets — patchy data; most secondary trading is on DEXs or OTC.
  3. Private chain deployments — JPMorgan Onyx, Goldman Sachs GS DAP, and other private blockchain RWA platforms do not publish TVL or transaction data.
  4. Cross-chain aggregate data — difficult to sum activity across Ethereum, Solana, Stellar, Avalanche, Base, etc.
