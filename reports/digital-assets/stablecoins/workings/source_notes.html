Stablecoins — Source Notes

Primary Sources

CryptoSlate

  • Reports $27.6T annual stablecoin transfer volume for 2024
  • Cites CEX.IO research and on-chain data
  • Good for headline figures; less granular than specialised analytics
  • Confidence: 🟢 High (cross-validated by multiple sources)

TRM Labs — 2025 Crypto Adoption and Stablecoin Usage Report

  • Comprehensive stablecoin market analysis
  • Provides USDC vs USDT volume share breakdown (70% vs 25%)
  • Active address data (USDT 67%, USDC 27%)
  • URL: https://www.trmlabs.com/reports-and-whitepapers/2025-crypto-adoption-and-stablecoin-usage-report
  • Confidence: 🟢 High

Visa Onchain Analytics Dashboard

  • Gold standard for ADJUSTED stablecoin volume
  • Strips out bot activity, HFT, bridge relay, treasury management
  • $2.5T in payment-like activity (12 months to May 2024)
  • Methodology is somewhat opaque but credible
  • URL: https://visaonchainanalytics.com/transactions
  • Confidence: 🟡 Medium (adjusted figure, methodology not fully public)

Artemis Analytics

  • Cross-chain stablecoin analytics (market cap, volumes, transfers, addresses)
  • Covers 50+ stablecoins across 20+ chains
  • URL: https://app.artemisanalytics.com/stablecoins
  • Confidence: 🟡 Medium

Bloomberg

  • Reports stablecoin transactions rose to $33T record (including early 2025)
  • Led by USDC
  • URL: https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-01-08/stablecoin-transactions-rose-to-record-33-trillion-led-by-usdc
  • Confidence: 🟢 High (though paywalled)

Data Gaps

  1. Transaction count by stablecoin — volume in USD is well-tracked; number of transactions is less consistently reported
  2. Geographic distribution — hard to determine what % of stablecoin volume is remittances vs DeFi vs speculation
  3. Tron-specific data — Tron is a major USDT chain but has less coverage in Western analytics platforms
  4. Pre-2022 volume — stablecoin tracking infrastructure was less mature; earlier figures are estimates

Cross-Validation

  • CryptoSlate $27.6T aligns with Bloomberg's report of rising volumes
  • TRM Labs market share data aligns with on-chain address analysis
  • Visa's adjusted $5.7T is consistent with Artemis payment analysis ($2.5T for partial year, scaled up)
  • Market cap of ~$205B is on-chain verifiable across all chains
