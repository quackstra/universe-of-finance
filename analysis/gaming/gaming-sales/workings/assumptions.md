# Digital Game Sales & Subscriptions — Assumptions

## A1. Scope Definition

"Digital game sales & subscriptions" includes:
- Full digital game purchases (PC, console, mobile premium)
- DLC and expansion pack purchases (distinct from microtransactions — these are one-time content additions)
- Subscription service payments (Game Pass, PS Plus, EA Play, Nintendo Switch Online, Ubisoft+)
- Subscription renewals (monthly or annual — each renewal = one transaction)
- Season passes and premium editions

Excluded:
- In-game microtransactions (covered in gaming-microtx)
- Physical game sales (declining; <5% of market)
- Gaming hardware purchases
- In-game advertising revenue
- Free-to-play game downloads (no transaction)
- Game streaming services that are bundled (e.g., PS Now folded into PS Plus)

## A2. Revenue Segmentation

From Newzoo total ($182.7B) minus microtransaction revenue ($114B) minus physical ($8.5B):
- **Digital game sales + subscriptions + DLC: ~$60.2B**

- PC digital game sales (Steam, Epic, others): ~$21B
- Console digital game sales: ~$28.5B (console $43.5B × 84% digital - ~$8B console IAP portion)

## A3. Transaction Count Methodology

### Full Game Purchases
- Average selling price (ASP) varies dramatically by platform and sale timing
- PC: ~$25 (heavy discounting on Steam sales; many indie games $5-15)
- Console: ~$45 (higher base prices, fewer deep discounts)
- Mobile premium: ~$5 (most paid mobile games are $0.99-$9.99)
- DLC: ~$15 (wide range from $2.99 map packs to $39.99 expansions)
- Weighted blended ASP across all: **~$28**

### Subscription Transactions
Each subscription payment (monthly/annual renewal) = one transaction.
- Monthly subscribers: 12 transactions/year per subscriber
- Annual subscribers: 1 transaction/year per subscriber
- Estimated monthly/annual split: 40% monthly / 60% annual (industry pattern)
- Average transactions per subscriber per year: 0.4 × 12 + 0.6 × 1 = **5.4**

## A5. Double-Counting Flag

Like microtransactions, digital game purchases are paid via card/wallet and overlap with:
- Consumer Card Payments
- E-commerce Payments
- Digital Wallet volumes

Flagged but not deduplicated at this category level.

## A6. Growth Rate Assumptions

### Historical Revenue Growth
- 2019-2020: +25% (COVID + digital shift acceleration)
- 2020-2021: -8% (post-COVID normalization; fewer blockbuster releases)
- 2021-2022: -5% (continued correction; inflation impact)
- 2022-2023: +8% (recovery; strong release slate)
- 2023-2024: +6% (steady growth; subscription maturation)

### Projection Assumptions
- Baseline CAGR 2024-2035: 5% (digital shift near-complete; subscription growth moderates)
- High: 8% (cloud gaming expansion; emerging market digital adoption)
- Conservative: 2% (market saturation; subscription fatigue)
