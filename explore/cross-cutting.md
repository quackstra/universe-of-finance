# Cross-Cutting Analysis

Analyses that span all 29 categories, examining the global financial system as a whole rather than any single sector.

## Overlap & De-duplication

- [Overlap Matrix](../analysis/OVERLAP_MATRIX.html) — Maps double-counting across all category pairs. The key input for converting gross TPS (~86,900) to the de-duplicated Big Number (~73,750).
- [Confidence Scorecard](../analysis/CONFIDENCE_SCORECARD.html) — 0--100 confidence scores for every category estimate, with explicit methodology for each rating.

## Peak & Stress

- [Peak TPS](../analysis/PEAK_TPS.html) — When does the world's financial infrastructure hit maximum load? Calendar co-occurrence analysis for flash crashes on busy quarter-ends.
- [Scenarios](../analysis/SCENARIOS.html) — Stress tests: recession (-0.3%), pandemic (+18--28%), crypto winter (-3.0%). Trading surges offset payment declines.

## Lifecycle & Freshness

- [Sunset Watch](../analysis/SUNSET_WATCH.html) — Declining categories tracked over time. Growth outpaces decline 38:1, but ATM withdrawals are in structural decline.
- [Data Freshness](../analysis/DATA_FRESHNESS.html) — Source staleness tracker. Flags categories where underlying data is aging and estimates may drift.

## Time-Series

- [TPS Time-Series](../analysis/timeseries/TIMESERIES.html) — 2015--2025 annual TPS for all 29 categories. Global gross TPS grew 6.7x in a decade.
- [MEST Time-Series](../analysis/timeseries/MEST_TIMESERIES.html) — Historical MEST progression, tracking how the state-change burden evolves alongside transaction growth.

## MEST

- [MEST Advantage](../analysis/MEST_ADVANTAGE.html) — DLT compression thesis: crypto achieves 60--80% fewer state changes per economic event compared to traditional rails, with cost modeling.
