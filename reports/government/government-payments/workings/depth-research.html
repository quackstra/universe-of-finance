Why +10 points? The depth research provided hard government data for the three most transparent systems (US IRS, India GSTN/DBT, UK DWP). These anchor points cover ~45% of global GDP and allow independent validation. The IRS Data Book's 266M returns + 4.6B information returns + $5.1T collections provides an unprecedented level of detail for the US component. India's 7.4B DBT transactions alone proves that a single country's benefits system can generate billions of annual transactions.

What would move to 70+: 1. China State Administration of Taxation publishing filing/payment counts 2. EU ViDA (VAT in the Digital Age) implementation data showing per-invoice reporting volumes 3. IMF or World Bank publishing a cross-country government payment transaction database 4. Sub-Saharan Africa digital government payment data becoming available
