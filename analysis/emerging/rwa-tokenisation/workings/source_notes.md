## Data Gaps

1. **On-chain RWA transaction counts** — no aggregator tracks this specifically. Our estimates are derived from TVL ratios.
2. **Secondary market trading volume** for tokenised assets — patchy data; most secondary trading is on DEXs or OTC.
3. **Private chain deployments** — JPMorgan Onyx, Goldman Sachs GS DAP, and other private blockchain RWA platforms do not publish TVL or transaction data.
4. **Cross-chain aggregate data** — difficult to sum activity across Ethereum, Solana, Stellar, Avalanche, Base, etc.
